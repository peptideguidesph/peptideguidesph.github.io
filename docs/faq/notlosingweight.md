# Not Losing Weight on 2.5mg Tirzepatide?

You started tirzepatide at **2.5mg and haven't seen weight loss yet.** This is **completely normal** and doesn't mean the medicine isn't working.

Here's why:

## ‚úÖ Why You Might Not Be Losing Weight Yet

**2.5mg is just the starting dose.** Here's the real deal:

- **Your body is adjusting** ‚Üí The first dose is low to let your body get used to the medicine and reduce side effects
- **You need a higher dose** ‚Üí Most people don't see real weight loss until they reach **5mg, 7.5mg, or higher**
- **Everyone progresses at different speeds** ‚Üí Some people feel effects at 2.5mg, but most need to go up
- **It takes time** ‚Üí Even at the right dose, weight loss usually shows after **4-12 weeks**, not immediately
- **Your body is still adjusting** ‚Üí Appetite suppression gets stronger as you keep using it
